About TechGirls

Empowering 4th & 5th grade girls in Howard County through hands-on STEM since 2022

Our Mission

TechGirls is a free after-school youth enrichment program under CAPA-HC Achieve Success Together (AST).

We promote gender equity in STEM by providing hands-on workshops, mentorship, and real-world experiences for 4th and 5th grade girls in Howard County.

What We Believe

  • 🌟 Every girl deserves access to quality STEM education
  • 👩‍🔬 Girls can be confident leaders in science and technology
  • 🤝 Strong mentorship creates lasting impact
