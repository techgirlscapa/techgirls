About TechGirls

Empowering 4th and 5th grade girls in Howard County through hands-on STEM education and mentorship.

Our Mission

TechGirls is a youth enrichment program under CAPA-HC Achieve Success Together (AST). We promote gender equity in STEM by providing free after-school workshops for elementary school girls.

Since 2022, we have grown to serve dozens of girls across multiple schools in Howard County, combining hands-on STEM learning with social-emotional development and mentorship from high school students.

What We Offer

  • ✓ Weekly 1.5-hour STEM workshops
  • ✓ Hands-on projects and experiments
  • ✓ Field trips to places like NASA
  • ✓ Graduation ceremony each semester
