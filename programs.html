Our Programs

Fun, hands-on STEM activities for 4th & 5th grade girls

Hands-on STEM Activities

  • 🛠️ Construct a prosthetic hand
  • 🌉 Make a bridge from popsicle sticks
  • ⚡ Tinker with Snap Circuits
  • 🌋 Make a baking soda volcano
  • 🎮 Games, Kahoot & more!

Program Details

  • Duration: 10 weeks per semester
  • Time: 1.5 hours weekly after school
  • Location: Various elementary schools in Howard County
  • Taught by: Trained high school tutors
  • Cost: FREE!
