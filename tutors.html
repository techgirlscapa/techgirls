TechGirls is a free after-school STEM program for 4th and 5th grade girls in Howard County, run by CAPA-HC Achieve Success Together.

As a high school tutor, you will teach fun, hands-on STEM lessons, mentor young girls, and help them build confidence in science and technology. You'll gain valuable teaching, leadership, and mentorship experience while making a real impact.

Why Become a TechGirls Tutor?

💰 Payment or Service Hours

Get paid or earn valuable community service hours.

🏆 Leadership Experience

Develop teaching and mentorship skills that look great on college applications.

❤️ Real Impact

Help close the gender gap in STEM by inspiring young girls.
